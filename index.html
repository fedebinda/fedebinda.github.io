About

I am currently an Associate Professor at the Department of Mathematics of the University of Milan. My research focuses on arithmetic and algebraic geometry, with a particular interest in algebraic K-theory, p-adic cohomologies, derived algebraic geometry, and motivic homotopy theory.

Before returning to Milan, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Regensburg within the SFB 1085 "Higher Invariants" research group. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Duisburg-Essen under the supervision of Marc N. Levine.

Since September 2023, I am an elected member of the K-theory Foundation.
